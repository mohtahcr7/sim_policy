Sima - Privacy Policy

Introduction

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. Sima is designed to work completely offline and does not collect or store any personal information.

Information We Collect

Sima does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All data (such as favorite names or notes) is stored locally on your device only.

How We Use Data

Since we do not collect any data, we do not use, analyze, or process any personal information.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. The share feature is only activated when the user chooses to share content manually.

Internet Usage

Sima does not require an internet connection. All features are fully available offline.

Permissions

Sima does not request any sensitive permissions. Sharing is only used when explicitly triggered by the user.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, data security depends on your device's security. We do not store any data on external servers.

Children's Privacy

Sima is suitable for all ages and does not collect any data from children or any users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
